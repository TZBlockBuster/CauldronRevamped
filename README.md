# Cauldron Overhaul

**Revitalize the cauldron with new fluids, mixing & brewing mechanics, and exciting items!**

This mod brings new life into Minecraft's cauldron by expanding its functionality far beyond water, lava, and powder snow. Now, you can fill cauldrons with **honey**, **slime**, and even **potions**! Potions poured into a cauldron will automatically mix, creating a powerful new **Mixed Potion** with combined effects.

## New Items
- **Slime Bucket**: Scoop up slime blocks or fill cauldrons with gooey slime.
- **Honey Bucket**: Harvest and store honey for cauldron use.
- **Slime Bottle**: Transports slime around. (More to come)
- **Lava Bottle**: Transports bits of lava around. (More to come)
- **Milk Bottle**: Stores milk for cauldron use, can be obtained by milking a cow. It can also be used to grow baby cows.

## Key Features
- Fill cauldrons with honey, slime, milk, or potions alongside existing fluids.
- **Potion Mixing**: Combine multiple potions in a cauldron to brew a unique **Mixed Potion**—experiment with effects for custom brews!
- Interact with cauldrons using the new buckets and bottles for seamless fluid management.
- **Cauldron Brewing**: You can brew potions within cauldrons. Place a campfire below to heat it up and drop the desired ingredients into the cauldron. Using the shift-key, you can see what's in your cauldron.

## More Features
- **Dispensable Sticky Blocks**: As there are now Slime and Honey Buckets, you use them with Dispensers to place and break them. I think this could be useful in some redstone contraptions. (Feel free to send me screenshots / videos of your creations, to use on the mod page)
- **Milk Cauldrons**: The same way you get effects from brewing cauldrons by entering them, you can also get the effects removed by entering a milk cauldron. This could be useful for removing bad effects very quickly. (Or even protecting yourself from bad effects by standing in a cauldron, for example, while fighting wither skeletons)
- **Lava Cauldron Levels**: Vanilla somehow is very inconsistent with lava cauldrons. They are the only cauldrons that don't have levels.

## Planned Features
- **Expanded Bottle Uses**: More ways to utilize slime and lava bottles in crafting and mechanics.
- **Honey Milk**: A new type of milk that allows you to remove only bad effects.
- **Mixed Potion Names**: Currently, you always get a "Mixed Potion" I want to add unique names based on some useful mixtures. (For example: Strength + Regeneration = Potion of Combat; Strength + Night Vision = Potion of the Dungeon Crawler; and so on)

This mod is in active development—expect more fluid interactions, recipes, and surprises ahead!
