# Cauldron Overhaul

**Revitalize the humble cauldron with new fluids, mixing mechanics, and exciting items!**

This mod breathes new life into Minecraft's cauldron by expanding its functionality far beyond water, lava, and powder snow. Now, you can fill cauldrons with **honey**, **slime**, and even **potions**! Potions poured into a cauldron will automatically mix together, creating a powerful new **Mixed Potion** with combined effects.

## New Items
- **Slime Bucket**: Scoop up slime blocks or fill cauldrons with gooey slime.
- **Honey Bucket**: Harvest and store honey for cauldron use.
- **Slime Bottle**: Transports slime around. (More to come)
- **Lava Bottle**: Transports bits of lava around. (More to come)

## Key Features
- Fill cauldrons with honey, slime, or potions alongside existing fluids.
- **Potion Mixing**: Combine multiple potions in a cauldron to brew a unique **Mixed Potion**—experiment with effects for custom brews!
- Interact with cauldrons using the new buckets and bottles for seamless fluid management.

## Planned Features
- **Brewing in Cauldrons**: Turn your cauldron into a full brewing station.
- **Milk Support**: Add milk to cauldrons for new recipe possibilities.
- **Expanded Bottle Uses**: More ways to utilize slime and lava bottles in crafting and mechanics.

This mod is in active development—expect more fluid interactions, recipes, and surprises ahead!
